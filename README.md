# Ice: Video Game Market Success Analysis

## About

Working for the online store "Ice," this project analyzes historical data from 1980 to 2016 to identify patterns that determine a video game's success. By evaluating platform lifecycles, global sales distributions, and the impact of critic/user reviews, we provide data-driven recommendations for marketing campaigns and inventory planning for the upcoming 2017 season.


## Technical Highlights

· **Data Preprocessing**: Standardized column names, handled missing values in critical fields like `Year_of_Release`, and converted "TBD" (To Be Determined) ratings to NaN for statistical integrity.

· **Platform Lifespan Analysis**: Identified that new platforms typically take **10 years** to fade, with the "peak" period occurring approximately **5 years** after launch.

· **Regional User Profiling**: Analyzed market preferences across North America (NA), Europe (EU), and Japan (JP), highlighting distinct differences in platform and genre popularity (e.g., the dominance of handhelds in Japan).

· **Impact of Reviews**: Quantified the correlation between critic scores and sales, utilizing scatter plots to visualize how professional ratings influence commercial performance.

· **Hypothesis Testing**: Conducted T-tests to verify if average user ratings differ between the Xbox One and PC platforms, and between Action and Sports genres.
